ABOUT PROJECT
Welcome To Mood

A revolutionary project that brings a new experience to the world of machine learning to help you understand and measure different levels of emotions. Mood allows users to quickly detect happy, sad or angry from uploaded images, text or sounds, making understanding emotions more accessible to everyone.

With a friendly and intuitive user interface, Mood gives users the flexibility to convey emotions through various forms of data. You can upload meaningful images, enter text that provides insight, or record sounds that reflect how you feel. Mood's intelligent system then works with the latest machine learning technology to recognize and analyze the emotional expressions contained in the content.

Mood not only provides real-time analysis results, but also presents clear and easy-to-understand visualizations. Key features of the project include fast and accurate analysis of various types of data, making Mood a very useful tool for understanding and responding to emotions.

With the support of the latest technology, a user-friendly interface, and guaranteed data security, Mood comes as a solution that is accessible to all. Let's explore and understand the world of emotions together through Mood. Welcome to a new era of deeper understanding of emotions!
